DEAwakening is built on presence, depth, and human connection. The core intention is not surface inspiration but embodied change that can be integrated into real life.

David Biddle's work combines therapeutic precision, energetic awareness, and practical structure. The process invites people to reconnect with their body intelligence, emotional truth, and personal clarity.

The role of the guide is to orient, simplify, and help each person take a real next step: understand the approach, choose a suitable event, explore the book, or start a direct conversation through contact.
