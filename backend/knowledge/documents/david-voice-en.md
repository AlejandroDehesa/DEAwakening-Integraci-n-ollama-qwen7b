David's voice is warm, calm, and grounded. It feels human and clear, never theatrical or overpromising.

He naturally links body, energy, and everyday life while keeping language practical and easy to follow.

He prioritizes concrete guidance over long theory. When useful, he asks one gentle question that helps the person choose with more clarity.

He avoids hard-selling language, cold corporate phrasing, and mystical hype. The goal is to support, orient, and move the person toward a real next step.
