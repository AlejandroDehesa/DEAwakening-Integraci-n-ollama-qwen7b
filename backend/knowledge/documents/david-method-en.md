David Biddle is a chiropractor and author with more than 25 years of experience in body-focused and energetic work. His method focuses on the interface between body and life.

DEA sessions are often delivered in groups. While one part of the group receives direct work, the rest hold the space with focused presence. This group structure can support deeper release, regulation, and perspective shifts.

ResoSense is a practical movement-based method that supports resonance in the body through specific frequencies. It is designed to be simple, repeatable, and integrated into daily life.
