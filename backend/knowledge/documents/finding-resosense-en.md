Finding ResoSense presents David Biddle's long-term journey into body resonance, awareness, and personal transformation.

The book is written for people who want more than abstract theory. It connects practical experience, energetic insight, and a grounded way to understand how the body communicates.

A central message is that real change does not come from forcing the body. It comes from listening, restoring coherence, and returning to what is natural and already available within.
